Pricing

Health Advice

We care about your well-being—unlike others in the trade. In an industry so physically demanding, we focus on stretching corrective muscular imbalance with morning qigong stretching and power poses:

  • Join our morning qigong sessions to loosen up and balance your body.
  • Strike power poses before work to boost energy and confidence.
  • Stay hydrated—bring a water bottle to site.

Meditations

Relax and recharge with these simple meditations to keep your mind strong:

  • 5-minute breathing: Inhale for 4, hold for 4, exhale for 4.
  • Focus on the present: Notice the weight of your tools.

Tax Help

We’ll support you with tax basics so you’re not left struggling:

  • Keep receipts for tools and travel.
  • File self-assessment early to avoid stress.
  • Ask us for help with forms!
